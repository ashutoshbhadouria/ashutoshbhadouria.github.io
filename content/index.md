hello, there! my name is Ashutosh (Ashu), and I am a PhD candidate at Purdue University in the field of [chemical engineering](https://www2.aiche.org/k-12/what-do-chemical-engineers-do).

I carry an inquisitive attitude and take pleasure in small and beautiful things that life offers. When not busy suffering through grad school, I enjoy spending time with my friends, playing badminton, and catching up with science fiction movies/shows. 

thank you for taking the time to visit this website, and stay tuned for regular (or maybe not-so-regular updates) in the near future...cheers!
